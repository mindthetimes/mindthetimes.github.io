## What is StepMania?
StepMania is a free dance and rhythm game for Windows, Mac, and Linux. The project currently lives on at their website and has their own Github releases page.

I started with using Stepmania 3.9 for its light capacity, and a standalone non-install version of that is on archive.org as [StepMania 3.9+ ReduxFull](https://archive.org/details/StepMania3.9ReduxFull). A good idea to start with if you don’t want the software to do a lot of heavy lifting at first.
